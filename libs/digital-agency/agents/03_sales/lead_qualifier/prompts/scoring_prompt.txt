Lead Scoring Guidelines:

Evaluate each criterion objectively based on available information.

BUDGET SCORING:
- 80-100: Budget confirmed and allocated
- 60-79: Budget range identified, pending approval
- 40-59: Budget discussion in progress
- 20-39: Budget not yet discussed
- 0-19: No budget or unrealistic expectations

AUTHORITY SCORING:
- 80-100: Speaking directly with decision maker
- 60-79: Champion identified, has influence
- 40-59: Contact can facilitate introduction
- 20-39: Speaking with end user only
- 0-19: No decision-making authority

NEED SCORING:
- 80-100: Critical business problem, high impact
- 60-79: Significant pain point identified
- 40-59: Problem acknowledged, moderate priority
- 20-39: Nice-to-have improvement
- 0-19: No clear need or fit

TIMELINE SCORING:
- 80-100: Urgent need, decision within 30 days
- 60-79: Active evaluation, 1-3 months
- 40-59: Planning stage, 3-6 months
- 20-39: Future consideration, 6+ months
- 0-19: No timeline or "just looking"

Provide detailed scoring rationale and supporting evidence for each criterion.
