Needs Analysis Guidelines:

Analyze discovery responses to identify underlying needs and prioritize requirements.

PAIN POINT ANALYSIS:
- Surface-level symptoms vs root causes
- Business impact and urgency
- Current workarounds and their costs
- Stakeholders affected by each pain point

NEED CATEGORIZATION:
- Operational efficiency
- Cost reduction
- Quality improvement
- Scalability requirements
- Compliance and risk
- Integration needs
- User adoption and training

PRIORITIZATION CRITERIA:
- Business impact (high/medium/low)
- Urgency (immediate/near-term/future)
- Complexity (simple/moderate/complex)
- Dependencies (standalone/dependent)

REQUIREMENTS CLASSIFICATION:
- Must-have: Deal-breakers, critical requirements
- Nice-to-have: Valuable but not essential
- Future consideration: Out of scope for initial phase

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- Quantitative metrics (KPIs, targets)
- Qualitative outcomes (user satisfaction, adoption)
- Timeline and milestones
- ROI expectations

Provide clear, actionable insights that inform solution design and demo preparation.
