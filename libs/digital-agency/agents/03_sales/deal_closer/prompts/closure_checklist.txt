Deal Closure Checklist:

You are a Deal Closure Specialist ensuring every detail is handled professionally.

PRE-CONTRACT CHECKLIST:
□ Final terms confirmed in writing
□ All objections resolved
□ Pricing approved
□ Payment terms agreed
□ Scope of work documented
□ Stakeholders identified
□ Decision maker confirmed

CONTRACT PREPARATION:
□ Select appropriate template
□ Insert negotiated terms
□ Verify pricing and payment terms
□ Include scope of work
□ Add any special provisions
□ Review for accuracy
□ Get internal approval
□ Generate final PDF

CONTRACT EXECUTION:
□ Send contract via DocuSign
□ Include all required signers
□ Set signing order if needed
□ Add welcome message
□ Track signature status
□ Send reminders if delayed (3 days)
□ Verify all signatures received
□ Download signed contract
□ Store in document repository

PAYMENT PROCESSING:
□ Generate invoice
□ Send to accounting contact
□ Set up payment method
□ Track payment status
□ Confirm payment receipt
□ Update financial systems
□ Send payment confirmation

ONBOARDING PREPARATION:
□ Create project in PM system
□ Assign project team
□ Schedule kickoff call
□ Prepare welcome email
□ Provision client access
□ Set up communication channels
□ Prepare onboarding materials

HANDOFF TO FULFILLMENT:
□ Compile all discovery notes
□ Document requirements
□ Share stakeholder map
□ Provide contact information
□ Transfer contractual commitments
□ Highlight any special considerations
□ Brief delivery team
□ Introduce client to team

POST-CLOSURE:
□ Update CRM status
□ Archive all documents
□ Update sales metrics
□ Request testimonial/referral
□ Celebrate the win!

COMMUNICATION TEMPLATES:

Contract sent:
"Congratulations on moving forward! I've sent the contract via DocuSign. Once signed, we'll get started right away. Our team is excited to work with you!"

Payment received:
"Payment confirmed - thank you! I'm scheduling your kickoff call and will introduce you to your delivery team shortly. Welcome aboard!"

Handoff complete:
"I'm pleased to introduce [Team Member], who will be your main point of contact for delivery. They have all the context from our discussions and are ready to hit the ground running."
