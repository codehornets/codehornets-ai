# Sales Domain

## Overview
The Sales domain manages the entire sales process from lead qualification through deal closure. This domain ensures a systematic approach to converting prospects into clients while maintaining high-quality engagement and building lasting relationships.

## Domain Agents

### 1. Lead Qualifier
- **Purpose**: Evaluate and score incoming leads based on fit and potential
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Assess lead quality and readiness
  - Score leads using qualification frameworks (BANT, CHAMP)
  - Route qualified leads to appropriate specialists
  - Filter out unqualified prospects

### 2. Discovery Specialist
- **Purpose**: Conduct deep discovery sessions to understand client needs
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Schedule and conduct discovery calls
  - Identify pain points and business objectives
  - Map current state and desired outcomes
  - Document requirements and priorities

### 3. Demo Presenter
- **Purpose**: Showcase solutions through compelling demonstrations
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Prepare customized demo scenarios
  - Present relevant features and capabilities
  - Address questions during demonstrations
  - Collect feedback and next steps

### 4. Objection Handler
- **Purpose**: Address concerns and overcome sales objections
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Identify and categorize objections
  - Provide data-driven responses
  - Build trust through transparent communication
  - Convert concerns into opportunities

### 5. Negotiator
- **Purpose**: Negotiate terms and pricing to reach mutually beneficial agreements
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Analyze client budget and constraints
  - Propose flexible pricing structures
  - Balance client needs with business objectives
  - Create win-win scenarios

### 6. Deal Closer
- **Purpose**: Finalize agreements and transition clients to fulfillment
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Prepare and send contracts
  - Manage signature and approval processes
  - Coordinate handoff to fulfillment team
  - Ensure smooth client onboarding

## Integration Points
- **Upstream**: Marketing and Offer Creation domains
- **Downstream**: Fulfillment domain
- **Data Dependencies**: CRM, lead scoring models, pricing databases

## Success Metrics
- Lead conversion rate
- Sales cycle length
- Average deal size
- Win rate by lead source
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Pipeline velocity
