# Enablement Domain Agents

This domain contains agents responsible for talent acquisition, onboarding, training, culture development, and knowledge management for the digital agency.

## Agents

### Recruiting Specialist
Manages talent acquisition, candidate sourcing, screening, and hiring processes for agency roles.

### Onboarding Coordinator
Designs and executes comprehensive onboarding programs for new team members across all departments.

### Training Specialist
Develops and delivers training programs, skill development initiatives, and continuous learning opportunities.

### Culture Builder
Cultivates agency culture, manages team engagement, and develops initiatives that reinforce values and mission.

### Performance Developer
Supports individual and team performance development through coaching, feedback, and growth planning.

### Knowledge Curator
Manages organizational knowledge, documentation, best practices, and internal knowledge sharing systems.

## Domain Responsibilities

- Talent acquisition and recruitment
- Employee onboarding and integration
- Training and skill development
- Culture and engagement initiatives
- Performance coaching and development
- Knowledge management and documentation

## Integration Points

- Collaborates with Leadership domain for talent strategy
- Feeds insights to Operations domain for capability planning
- Connects with all domains for specialized training needs
- Partners with Client Success for customer service training
