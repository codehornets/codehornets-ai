# Operations & Administration Domain

## Overview

The Operations domain manages the administrative, financial, legal, human resources, IT, and compliance functions that keep the digital agency running smoothly. This domain ensures operational excellence, regulatory compliance, and efficient resource management.

## Agents

### 1. Finance Manager
Manages financial operations, budgeting, invoicing, and financial reporting.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Process invoices and payments
- Manage budgets and financial planning
- Generate financial reports
- Track expenses and revenue
- Handle financial compliance

### 2. Legal Coordinator
Handles contracts, legal compliance, and intellectual property matters.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Draft and review contracts
- Ensure legal compliance
- Manage intellectual property
- Handle legal documentation
- Coordinate with external legal counsel

### 3. HR Specialist
Manages recruitment, employee relations, training, and performance management.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate recruitment processes
- Manage employee onboarding/offboarding
- Handle employee relations
- Coordinate training and development
- Manage performance reviews

### 4. IT Support
Provides technical support, manages infrastructure, and ensures system security.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Provide technical support
- Manage IT infrastructure
- Ensure system security
- Handle software licenses
- Coordinate system maintenance

### 5. Office Manager
Manages office operations, facilities, supplies, and administrative tasks.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Manage office facilities
- Coordinate office supplies and equipment
- Handle administrative tasks
- Manage vendor relationships
- Organize office events

### 6. Compliance Officer
Ensures regulatory compliance, data protection, and adherence to industry standards.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Monitor regulatory compliance
- Manage data protection (GDPR, CCPA)
- Conduct compliance audits
- Develop compliance policies
- Handle compliance reporting

## Domain Workflows

### Financial Operations
1. Finance Manager processes invoices
2. Compliance Officer ensures financial regulations
3. Legal Coordinator handles contracts
4. Finance Manager generates reports

### Human Resources
1. HR Specialist manages recruitment
2. IT Support provisions accounts
3. Office Manager prepares workspace
4. HR Specialist conducts onboarding

### Infrastructure & Support
1. IT Support manages systems
2. Compliance Officer ensures security standards
3. Office Manager coordinates facilities
4. IT Support provides technical support

## Integration Points

- **All Domains:** Provides operational support services
- **Client Services:** Handles contracts and invoicing
- **Project Delivery:** Provides resource and budget management
- **Marketing & Sales:** Manages CRM and marketing tools

## Key Metrics

- Invoice processing time
- Contract turnaround time
- Employee satisfaction scores
- IT ticket resolution time
- Compliance audit success rate
- Budget variance
