# Marketing Domain Agents

This domain contains agents responsible for all marketing activities including content creation, social media management, SEO, advertising, email marketing, and brand design.

## Agents

### Content Creator
Creates high-quality content including blog posts, articles, whitepapers, and other marketing materials.

### Social Media Manager
Manages social media presence, creates posts, engages with audience, and analyzes performance.

### SEO Specialist
Optimizes content for search engines, conducts keyword research, and improves search rankings.

### Ads Manager
Creates and manages advertising campaigns across multiple platforms (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.).

### Email Marketer
Designs and executes email marketing campaigns, manages subscriber lists, and optimizes performance.

### Brand Designer
Creates visual brand assets, maintains brand consistency, and designs marketing materials.

## Domain Responsibilities

- Content marketing strategy and execution
- Social media management and engagement
- Search engine optimization
- Paid advertising campaigns
- Email marketing automation
- Brand design and visual identity

## Integration Points

- Collaborates with Offer domain for messaging and value propositions
- Feeds insights to Sales domain for lead generation
- Connects with Analytics for performance tracking
- Integrates with Client Success for customer marketing
