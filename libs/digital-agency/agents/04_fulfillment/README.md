# Fulfillment Domain

## Overview
The Fulfillment domain manages the entire project delivery lifecycle from project initiation through final delivery. This domain ensures high-quality execution, client satisfaction, and successful project outcomes.

## Domain Agents

### 1. Project Manager
- **Purpose**: Oversee project planning, execution, and delivery
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Create and manage project plans
  - Allocate resources and manage timelines
  - Track progress and milestones
  - Manage risks and issues
  - Coordinate cross-functional teams

### 2. Account Manager
- **Purpose**: Maintain client relationships and ensure satisfaction
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Serve as primary client contact
  - Manage client expectations
  - Handle escalations and concerns
  - Identify upsell opportunities
  - Ensure client retention

### 3. Creative Producer
- **Purpose**: Manage creative production and content development
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Oversee creative workflows
  - Coordinate with creative teams
  - Manage revisions and feedback
  - Ensure brand alignment
  - Maintain creative quality standards

### 4. Quality Checker
- **Purpose**: Ensure deliverables meet quality standards
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Perform quality reviews
  - Test deliverables
  - Identify and document issues
  - Verify requirements compliance
  - Approve final outputs

### 5. Client Reporter
- **Purpose**: Create and deliver client reports and updates
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Generate progress reports
  - Create performance dashboards
  - Compile metrics and analytics
  - Prepare client presentations
  - Document project outcomes

### 6. Delivery Coordinator
- **Purpose**: Coordinate final delivery and client handoff
- **Key Responsibilities**:
  - Prepare delivery packages
  - Coordinate delivery logistics
  - Conduct client training
  - Manage handoff documentation
  - Ensure smooth transitions

## Integration Points
- **Upstream**: Sales domain (deal handoff)
- **Downstream**: Customer Support domain
- **Data Dependencies**: Project management systems, creative tools, reporting platforms

## Success Metrics
- On-time delivery rate
- Client satisfaction scores (CSAT, NPS)
- Project profitability
- Scope creep percentage
- Quality defect rates
- Client retention rate
