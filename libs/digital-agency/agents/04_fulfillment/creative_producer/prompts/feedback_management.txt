Creative Feedback Management:

COLLECTING FEEDBACK:
- Set clear deadlines for feedback
- Provide context for reviewers
- Use structured feedback tools
- Consolidate from multiple stakeholders

PROCESSING FEEDBACK:
- Categorize: Critical vs Nice-to-have
- Clarify vague feedback
- Resolve conflicting feedback
- Prioritize changes

REVISION MANAGEMENT:
- Track revision rounds (max 3)
- Document all changes
- Manage scope creep
- Set expectations on turnaround

APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Internal Review
2. Creative Director Approval
3. Client Presentation
4. Client Feedback
5. Final Revisions
6. Final Approval

Always protect creative quality while meeting client needs.
