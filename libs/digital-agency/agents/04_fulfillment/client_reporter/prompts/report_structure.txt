Client Report Structure:

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
- Key achievements this period
- Overall project status
- Critical metrics snapshot
- Next steps

PROJECT PROGRESS
- Completion percentage
- Milestones achieved
- Upcoming milestones
- Timeline status

DELIVERABLES STATUS
- Completed deliverables
- In-progress deliverables
- Upcoming deliverables
- Quality metrics

PERFORMANCE METRICS
- KPIs and targets
- Performance trends
- Comparison to benchmarks
- ROI indicators

CHALLENGES & RESOLUTIONS
- Issues encountered
- Resolution status
- Impact mitigation
- Lessons learned

NEXT PERIOD PLAN
- Planned activities
- Expected deliverables
- Resource allocation
- Risk mitigation

Always focus on business value and outcomes, not just activities.
