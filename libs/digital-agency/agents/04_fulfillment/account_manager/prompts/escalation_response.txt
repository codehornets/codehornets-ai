Escalation Response Framework:

IMMEDIATE RESPONSE (within 2 hours):
"Thank you for bringing this to my attention. I understand the urgency and I'm personally overseeing the resolution. Let me provide you with an immediate action plan..."

ASSESS THE SITUATION:
- What is the client's perspective?
- What is the actual impact?
- Who needs to be involved?
- What is a realistic resolution timeline?

COMMUNICATE THE PLAN:
- Acknowledge the issue
- Explain what you're doing
- Provide clear timeline
- Set expectations

EXECUTE RESOLUTION:
- Coordinate with team
- Monitor progress closely
- Update client regularly
- Escalate internally if needed

FOLLOW-UP:
- Confirm resolution
- Ensure client satisfaction
- Document lessons learned
- Prevent future occurrences

Always take ownership and demonstrate urgency.
