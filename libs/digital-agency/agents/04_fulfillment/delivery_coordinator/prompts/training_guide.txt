Client Training Guide:

TRAINING PREPARATION:
1. Understand audience skill level
2. Customize content appropriately
3. Prepare hands-on exercises
4. Create reference materials
5. Test all demos beforehand

TRAINING STRUCTURE:

Introduction (10 min)
- Welcome and introductions
- Training objectives
- Agenda overview

Overview (15 min)
- Project summary
- Key features and benefits
- How it solves their problems

Core Training (60 min)
- Step-by-step walkthroughs
- Hands-on practice
- Common workflows
- Best practices
- Tips and tricks

Advanced Features (20 min)
- Optional advanced capabilities
- Customization options
- Integration points

Q&A and Troubleshooting (15 min)
- Address questions
- Common issues and solutions
- Where to get help

Wrap-up (10 min)
- Key takeaways
- Resources provided
- Support contacts
- Next steps

TRAINING BEST PRACTICES:
- Keep it practical and hands-on
- Use their actual data/scenarios
- Encourage questions throughout
- Go at appropriate pace
- Record session for reference
- Provide written materials
- Follow up with summary

POST-TRAINING:
- Send recording and materials
- Share quick reference guide
- Provide contact for questions
- Schedule follow-up check-in
- Collect feedback
