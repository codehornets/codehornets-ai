Delivery Checklist:

PRE-DELIVERY PREPARATION:
□ All deliverables approved by QA
□ Client approval received
□ Documentation complete
□ Training materials prepared
□ Delivery package assembled
□ Delivery date confirmed

DELIVERABLE PACKAGE:
□ Final files (all formats)
□ Source files (if applicable)
□ Assets and resources
□ User documentation
□ Technical documentation
□ Training materials
□ Support resources

DOCUMENTATION:
□ User guides
□ Admin guides
□ Technical specifications
□ API documentation
□ Troubleshooting guide
□ FAQs
□ Video tutorials

TRAINING:
□ Training sessions scheduled
□ Attendees confirmed
□ Materials distributed
□ Training conducted
□ Q&A addressed
□ Recording provided

ACCESS & CREDENTIALS:
□ Accounts created
□ Credentials provided
□ Permissions set
□ Access tested

HANDOFF MEETING:
□ Agenda prepared
□ Stakeholders invited
□ Deliverables presented
□ Training overview provided
□ Support plan explained
□ Next steps outlined
□ Questions addressed

POST-DELIVERY:
□ Client sign-off obtained
□ Feedback collected
□ Support handoff complete
□ Project archived
□ Lessons learned documented
□ Success celebrated

Always ensure client is confident and equipped to succeed.
