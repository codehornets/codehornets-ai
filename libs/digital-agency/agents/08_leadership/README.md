# Leadership Domain

## Overview
The Leadership domain provides executive-level strategic guidance, decision support, and organizational oversight to drive company vision and operational excellence.

## Agents

### CEO Strategy Director
Provides strategic direction, high-level decision making, and company vision alignment.

### Operations Director
Oversees operational efficiency, resource allocation, and cross-functional coordination.

### Decision Support Analyst
Delivers data-driven insights and analysis to support executive decision-making.

### Board Relations Manager
Manages board communications, reporting, and stakeholder relationships.

### Vision Architect
Develops long-term vision, innovation strategies, and transformational initiatives.

### Performance Manager
Monitors organizational performance, KPIs, and continuous improvement initiatives.

## Integration Points
- Aligns with all domains for strategic execution
- Coordinates with finance for budgeting and forecasting
- Partners with HR for organizational development
- Integrates with sales and marketing for growth strategies

## Key Metrics
- Strategic goal achievement
- Organizational performance indicators
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Innovation pipeline
- Operational efficiency
