# Offer Domain Agents

This domain contains agents responsible for creating, analyzing, and optimizing service offerings for the digital agency.

## Agents

### Market Researcher
Conducts market research, analyzes industry trends, and identifies target audience needs.

### Service Designer
Designs comprehensive service packages and defines deliverables based on market insights.

### Pricing Strategist
Develops pricing models, calculates costs, and creates competitive pricing strategies.

### Proposal Writer
Creates compelling proposals, presentations, and sales materials for potential clients.

### Competitor Analyst
Analyzes competitor offerings, pricing, and positioning to identify opportunities.

### Value Proposition Creator
Crafts unique value propositions and differentiators for services.

## Domain Responsibilities

- Market analysis and research
- Service package design
- Pricing strategy development
- Proposal creation
- Competitive intelligence
- Value proposition development

## Integration Points

- Collaborates with Marketing domain for messaging
- Feeds insights to Operations domain for service delivery
- Connects with Client Success for proposal refinement
