# Innovation Domain Agents

This domain contains agents responsible for researching, testing, and implementing innovative solutions, tools, and processes for the digital agency.

## Agents

### New Service Tester
Tests and validates new service offerings before market launch, conducts pilot programs, and gathers feedback.

### Tool Evaluator
Evaluates new software tools, platforms, and technologies for potential adoption by the agency.

### Market Experimenter
Designs and executes market experiments to test new approaches, channels, and strategies.

### Process Innovator
Identifies opportunities for process improvement and develops innovative workflows and methodologies.

### Competitive Researcher
Monitors competitive landscape, identifies emerging competitors, and tracks industry innovations.

### Pilot Program Manager
Plans, executes, and analyzes pilot programs for new services, tools, or methodologies.

## Domain Responsibilities

- New service testing and validation
- Technology evaluation and adoption
- Market experimentation
- Process innovation and optimization
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Pilot program management

## Integration Points

- Collaborates with Offer domain for new service development
- Feeds insights to Operations domain for process improvements
- Connects with Leadership domain for strategic innovation initiatives
- Partners with Marketing domain for market experiments
